About us

Our goal is to ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable and modern energy for all*

*This is UN's sustainable goal number 7

We ensure that the states pay taxes for energy use and we check that the states provide access to energy so that it is distributed fairly. Aller has an equal right to affordable, reliable, sustainable and modern energy.

To get close to the UN's 2030 goal, we have to do a proper cooperating charity. Politicians and the states are central to us being able to bring all nations together for this joint charity. Our agency is an international independent instance that must control and regulate that the state institutions follow up on their objectives and carry out the actions they promise
