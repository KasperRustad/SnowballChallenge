About us

We are the United People's Controllers. By the people for the people, for a sustainable future for all!
Our task is to get people to the states to pay taxes and let the authorities control the energy supply so that it becomes cheaper and hopefully free eventually.
Our UPC's today consist of a group of 20 international political scientists, lawyers, economists, researchers and environmental experts who are all passionate about a more sustainable world.
They have been given power by the UN to control that states perform their duties and can impose financial fines on states if they break their promises or do not follow up on their duties.
